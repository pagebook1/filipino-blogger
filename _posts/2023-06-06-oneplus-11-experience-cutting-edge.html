The OnePlus 11 sets a new benchmark for excellence, offering cutting-edge features and exceptional performance. From its stunning display and powerful processor to its professional-grade camera system and lightning-fast charging, the OnePlus 11 is designed to impress. Let's delve into its key specifications and explore why it stands out among flagship smartphones.



Unparalleled Display and Design:

The OnePlus 11 boasts a LTPO3 Fluid AMOLED display that showcases vivid colors and deep blacks. With a size of 6.7 inches and a 120Hz refresh rate, you'll experience smooth scrolling and fluid animations. Dolby Vision and HDR10+ support elevate your viewing experience, bringing content to life with vibrant colors and enhanced contrast. The display is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass Victus for added durability.


Under the sleek exterior lies a robust build. The OnePlus 11 features a glass front and back, with Gorilla Glass 5, and an aluminum frame. Its slim dimensions of 163.1 x 74.1 x 8.5 mm and weight of 205 g make it comfortable to hold and use.


Powerful Performance:

Equipped with the Qualcomm SM8550-AB Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chipset and an octa-core CPU, the OnePlus 11 delivers exceptional performance. The CPU configuration includes a combination of Cortex-X3, Cortex-A715, Cortex-A710, and Cortex-A510 cores, offering a balance between power and efficiency. The Adreno 740 GPU ensures smooth graphics rendering, enabling immersive gaming experiences and seamless multitasking.


Impressive Camera Capabilities:

The OnePlus 11 features a triple-camera system co-developed with Hasselblad, renowned for its exceptional image quality. The setup includes a 50 MP wide lens with f/1.8 aperture and optical image stabilization (OIS) for crisp and detailed photos. The 32 MP telephoto lens offers 2x optical zoom, and the 48 MP ultrawide lens captures expansive shots with its 115Ëš field of view. With Hasselblad Color Calibration, your photos will exhibit true-to-life colors and stunning accuracy.


Capture your world in stunning detail with the OnePlus 11's 8K video recording capabilities. Whether you're recording memorable moments or creating content, the OnePlus 11 ensures exceptional clarity and richness in your videos. Additionally, the 16 MP front-facing camera takes vibrant selfies with Auto-HDR and panorama modes.


Fast Charging and Advanced Features:

The OnePlus 11 comes with a non-removable 5000 mAh battery that provides all-day usage. The device supports 100W wired charging, allowing you to quickly recharge your phone and stay connected. With its innovative charging technology, you can reach 1-50% battery in just 10 minutes and achieve a full charge in 25 minutes.


In terms of connectivity, the OnePlus 11 supports 5G network capabilities, ensuring faster download and browsing speeds. It also features an under-display optical fingerprint sensor, accelerometer, gyro, proximity sensor, compass, and color spectrum sensor for an enhanced user experience.


The OnePlus 11 redefines smartphone excellence with its cutting-edge features and exceptional performance. From the LTPO3 Fluid AMOLED display and Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chipset to the Hasselblad-tuned camera system and 8K video recording capabilities, this flagship device delivers on all fronts. The 100W wired charging ensures quick power-ups, keeping you connected throughout the day. Experience the perfect blend of innovation, power, and style with the OnePlus 11.
