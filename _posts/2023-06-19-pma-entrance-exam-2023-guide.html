 If you're looking for a rewarding career that serves your country, becoming a cadet at the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) may be for you. PMA is a prestigious academy that trains aspiring professional military officers, and passing the PMA Entrance Exam is the primary requirement to become a cadet. In this article, we will take a closer look at the 2023 PMA Entrance Exam to keep you informed and increase your chances of passing.






PMAEE Schedule 2023


The PMA Entrance Exam is a highly competitive test that requires ample preparation and commitment. The PMA Office of Cadet Recruitment and Admission has announced the PMAEE's opening, starting May 1 and closing on August 1, 2023. It's recommended to apply early to give yourself more time to prepare and finalize requirements before the exam date. Moreover, the PMA will no longer accept walk-in and mail-in applications starting last year. Hence, aspiring cadets have only one way to apply, which is through the PMA's Online Application System. This system allows applicants to fill out their applications, upload required documents and other related materials needed for the application process.


Qualifications


The Philippine Military Academy is looking for the most qualified and deserving applicants that embody the true essence of an officer and a leader. These are the essential qualifications and requirements that aspiring applicants must fulfill:


- Must be a natural-born Filipino citizen

- Physically fit and of good moral character

- Single without dependents

- Passed the PMA Entrance Examination

- No Administrative or Criminal Case

- High School Graduate with at least 85% GPA

- Minimum height requirement of 5 feet for both males and females


How to Apply to PMA


PMA's Online Application System is an easy and convenient way to submit your application, requirements, and other related materials. Here's a simple guide to walk you through the application process:


Register and create an account through the PMA's online application portal.

Fill out the form and provide accurate information.

Upload the required documents and take note of the file size and relevant format.

Once done, submit your application form and wait for the validation of your application.

After validation, download and print your Exam Permit that serves as your entry pass to the examination hall.


PMA Entrance Exam Coverage


The admission test is challenging and requires a comprehensive understanding of different areas. Make sure to prepare and review for the following subjects included in the examination:


- Algebra and Geometry

- Grammar

- Composition

- Reading and Comprehension

- Verbal Reasoning

- Numerical Reasoning

- Pattern Analysis.

It's important to allocate ample time, at least three months, to review and practice your knowledge, either through your school's review courses or online resources such as free PMA Entrance Exam reviewers.


Medical Disqualifications


To be eligible for the training, applicants must pass the medical examination and meet specific requirements. Thus, take a glance at possible medical disqualifications to inform you beforehand:


- Colorblindness

- Any communicable disease

- Physical deformities

- Scoliosis

- Visible tattoos

- Other medical conditions as determined by the AFP Medical Board


What to do If You Pass the PMAEE?


After receiving your Exams Permit, take the PMA Entrance Exam on August 1, 2023, and wait for the results. Successful candidates will receive an official notification through mail, newspapers, and their website. They will schedule you for an interview and a physical examination at the Armed Forces of the Philippines Medical Center (formerly V. Luna). It's imperative to make sure you passed the PMA medical examination to continue and frequently check the PMA website for upcoming schedules and instructions.


In conclusion, the Philippine Military Academy is a well-respected institution that trains and develops military professionals to serve the country. Passing the PMA Entrance Exam is the primary requirement that ensures a cadet's eligibility for this exceptional opportunity. Henceforth, be prepared, review well, and visit the PMA's official website to stay informed with the latest updates and announcements.
