"Batang Quiapo" tells the story of two families whose lives intersect in the bustling Quiapo district of Manila. The first family is headed by Totoy (played by Arjo Atayde), a streetwise vendor who dreams of giving his family a better life. His wife, Liza (played by Janella Salvador), is a loving mother who supports Totoy's aspirations. The second family is led by the wealthy and influential Don Luis (played by Tirso Cruz III), whose daughter, Sofia (played by Jane de Leon), falls in love with Totoy's son, Pipo (played by Marco Gallo).
