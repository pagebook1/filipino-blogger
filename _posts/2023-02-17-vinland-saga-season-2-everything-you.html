Vinland Saga Season 2: Everything You Need to Know

In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about Vinland Saga Season 2, from its release date and plot to the cast and crew. So, let's get started!

First things first, when can we expect to see Vinland Saga Season 2? As of now, there is no official release date for the second season of the anime. Plot

The first season of Vinland Saga followed the story of Thorfinn, a young warrior who seeks revenge against Askeladd, the man who killed his father. Along the way, he becomes involved in a larger conflict between the Danish and English armies, as well as the larger conflict between pagans and Christians.

While there is no official plot synopsis for the second season, fans of the manga know what to expect. The second season will continue to follow the story of Thorfinn as he embarks on a new journey. Without giving away too much, the second season will see Thorfinn travel to Vinland, a legendary place that his father had once told him about. There, he will encounter new challenges and enemies, and his quest for revenge will take a backseat to new goals.

As for the cast, the original Japanese voice actors are expected to reprise their roles for the second season. This includes Shizuka Ishigami as Thorfinn, Naoya Uchida as Askeladd, and Yuuto Uemura as Canute.


Source Material

As mentioned earlier, Vinland Saga is based on a manga of the same name by Makoto Yukimura. The manga was first published in 2005 and is still ongoing, with over 24 volumes currently available.

The anime adaptation has been well received by fans of the manga, with many praising the series for its faithfulness to the source material. With the second season, fans can likely expect more of the same.
